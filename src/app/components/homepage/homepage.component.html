Medical specialty refers to a particular branch or field of medicine that focuses on a specific area of expertise. In the vast and diverse field of healthcare, medical specialties allow doctors to specialize in a particular organ system, disease type, age group, or medical procedure. These specialties encompass various disciplines, ranging from cardiology, neurology, and oncology to pediatrics, dermatology, and orthopedics, among many others.
